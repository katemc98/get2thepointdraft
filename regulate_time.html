Create family
guidelines &
a healthy online/
offline balance

HOW?

Set guidelines together...

No phones at
the dinner table
or in the bedroom
at night”

Children respond better when they have contributed.
Make a chart/ print out of the guidelines and stick it
on the fridge or somewhere visible as a reminder to
the whole family! Use positive praise or a reward
system to encourage participation.

Dont forget

You need to stick to the guidelines too. This will
ensure that your child takes them seriously.

Take action...

Do you
want to play
Football on
saturday?”

By arranging non-screen based activities, you are helping
to create a healthy online/ offline balance and teaching
your child that there are other fun ways to spend their time.
