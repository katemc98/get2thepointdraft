Always Be there
& be approachable

HOW?

Always remind them...

I’m here
for you no
matter what
& i won’t
get mad!”

Letting your child know that they can come to you without
any fear of you getting angry at them for their mistakes will
mean that they will be more inclined to come to you for help
in the future.

Remember...

Keep your cool
& find a solution;
consequences
can come later.

Re-assuring your child and finding a solution to the problem
should always be your first priority. It is a brave move for
them to share their problems with you, keeping your cool will
ensure that they continue to come to you for help in the future.
